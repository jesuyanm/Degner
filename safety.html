Safety First

Our commitment to zero incidents through comprehensive safety management and a culture where everyone truly cares about one another

Safety Excellence

Building a Culture of Safety

Safety is at the core of everything we do and is one of our central guiding principles. We promote and foster a culture where people truly care about one another and where everyone shares in our goal of zero incidents.

We believe that all entities affected by our operations are entitled to a safe and sustainable environment. At Degner Construction Incorporated we are committed to conducting all our activities in a manner that protects the health and safety of our employees, suppliers, customers, community and the environment.

We recognize that our ability to achieve and maintain the highest possible standards in health and safety and adherence to Occupational Health and Safety guidelines can only be achieved through active participation and conscious effort by everyone, everyday and in every task performed.

Our Health and Safety programs' emphasis is on continually improving our process and advancing our standards by implementing a rigorous training program customized to specific tasks across our organization. We routinely review our safety procedures as settings change, challenging norms and conventions all with the intention of improving our environment.

Our Mandate

To promote and sustain a safe work environment for everyone and continually improve our process.

Safety Programs

Comprehensive Safety Management

Fall Protection Program

Comprehensive fall protection protocols including proper harness use, rescue procedures, and 100% tie-off policies for all work at height. Regular inspection and maintenance of fall protection equipment ensures reliability when it matters most.

Excavation & Trenching Safety

Specialized protocols for deep utility excavation including soil classification, protective systems, atmospheric monitoring, and emergency egress procedures. Our team is trained in confined space entry and rescue operations.

Electrical Safety Program

Lockout/tagout procedures, electrical hazard identification, and safe work practices around overhead and underground utilities. All electrical work is performed by certified electricians following strict safety protocols.

Equipment Safety

Daily equipment inspections, operator certification requirements, and maintenance protocols for all heavy machinery. Specialized training for excavators, compactors, and lifting equipment used in municipal infrastructure projects.

Personal Protective Equipment

Mandatory PPE requirements including hard hats, safety glasses, high-visibility clothing, and specialized protective equipment for specific tasks. Regular training on proper use, inspection, and maintenance of PPE.

Environmental Health & Safety

Comprehensive environmental monitoring, hazardous material handling protocols, and emergency response procedures. Special attention to air quality in confined spaces and proper disposal of construction materials.

Safety Policies

Protocols & Procedures

Daily Safety Briefings

Every workday begins with a tailgate safety meeting covering specific hazards, weather conditions, and emergency procedures. These briefings ensure all team members are aware of potential risks and safety measures for the day's activities.

Incident Reporting & Investigation

Comprehensive incident reporting system with immediate investigation protocols. All near-misses and incidents are thoroughly analyzed to prevent recurrence and improve our safety procedures.

Contractor Safety Requirements

All subcontractors must meet our stringent safety standards including proof of safety training, insurance coverage, and compliance with our safety management system before being permitted on any project site.
