Safety First

At Degner Construction, we believe all entities affected by our operations are entitled to a safe and sustainable environment. We conduct every activity protecting the health and safety of our employees, suppliers, customers, community, and the environment — achieved through active participation and conscious effort by everyone, in every task performed. Our people are among the best trained in the industry, routinely participating in external safety committees and continually reviewing procedures to raise standards, not just meet them.

MG

Michelle Gregoire

NCSO · COR Peer Auditor · H&S Administrator
Safety Coordinator

Michelle's expertise in safety management and hazard analysis ensures that every Degner Construction site upholds the highest standards of health and safety. With her NCSO designation, COR Peer Auditor status, and credentials from both the Alberta Construction Safety Association and the University of New Brunswick, she oversees comprehensive safety programs, conducts thorough site inspections, and ensures full compliance with provincial and federal regulations. Michelle's dedication to protecting people makes her an essential part of our team.

In 2011, the Alberta Construction Safety Association presented Degner Construction Incorporated with the "Trailblazer" award — recognizing outstanding dedication to the enhancement of safety & health in the workplace.
