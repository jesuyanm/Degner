Safety First

At Degner Construction, we believe all entities affected by our operations are entitled to a safe and sustainable environment. We conduct every activity protecting the health and safety of our employees, suppliers, customers, community, and the environment — achieved through active participation and conscious effort by everyone, in every task performed.

In 2011, the Alberta Construction Safety Association presented Degner Construction Incorporated with the "Trailblazer" award — recognizing outstanding dedication to the enhancement of safety & health in the workplace.

Industry Memberships

  • Members in good standing — Workers Compensation Board (WCB)
  • Members — Alberta Construction Safety Association (ACSA)
  • Members — Urban Development Institute (UDI) Edmonton Region
  • Members — Alberta Roadbuilders & Heavy Construction Association (ARHCA)
  • Members — Edmonton Construction Association (ECA)
  • Active Regional Safety Committee (RSCM) member — Video & Poster Committees
  • Canadian Society of Safety Engineering Conference & Trade Show participant
  • Supporting Skills Canada — encouraging students into trades and construction

Daily Safety Briefings

Every workday begins with a tailgate safety meeting covering hazards, weather, and emergency procedures.

Incident Reporting & Investigation

All near-misses and incidents are immediately investigated and analyzed to prevent recurrence.

Contractor Safety Requirements

All subcontractors must meet our safety standards — training, insurance, and SMS compliance — before setting foot on site.
