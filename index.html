Our Foundation

Building Tomorrow's Infrastructure

At Degner Construction, we specialize in creating the essential infrastructure that supports thriving communities across Alberta

With decades of experience in municipal infrastructure, we understand that every project we complete becomes part of the foundation upon which families, businesses, and dreams are built. Our comprehensive approach combines cutting-edge technology with proven methodologies.

From water and sanitary systems to storm management facilities, we deliver infrastructure solutions that stand the test of time while meeting the evolving needs of modern communities.

Our promise is to build community value into every project while delivering professional expertise, exceptional customer service and quality construction

What We Do

Comprehensive Infrastructure Solutions

From planning to completion, we provide full-service municipal infrastructure development

General Contracting

General Contracting for all Aspects of Municipal Infrastructure Projects. We provide comprehensive project management and execution for complex municipal developments.

Water, Sanitary & Storm Services

Installation of Water, Sanitary and Storm Services. Our expert team ensures reliable, efficient systems that meet all municipal standards and regulations.
