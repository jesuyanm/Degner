Career Opportunities

Build Your Future with Degner Construction

Join a team that values professional growth, safety, and excellence. We're always looking for talented individuals who share our commitment to building Alberta's essential infrastructure.

Why Choose Degner Construction?

We offer more than just a job - we provide a career path with opportunities for growth, competitive benefits, and a supportive team environment.

Competitive Compensation

We offer competitive wages that reflect your skills and experience, with regular performance-based increases and advancement opportunities.

Comprehensive Benefits

Health and dental benefits after 3 months, plus additional perks that support your well-being and work-life balance.

Safety Training

Comprehensive safety training programs to ensure every team member goes home safely every day. Your safety is our top priority.

Professional Growth

Clear advancement opportunities with mentorship from experienced professionals and ongoing skills development programs.

Team Environment

Work with a professional and fun team that values collaboration, respect, and shared success on every project.

Industry Leadership

Be part of one of Edmonton's premier contractors, working on high-profile municipal infrastructure projects across Alberta.

Current Opportunities

We're currently recruiting for the following positions for the upcoming construction season. All positions offer competitive wages and comprehensive benefits.

Foreman

Leadership • Field Operations

Lead field crews in municipal infrastructure construction projects. Oversee daily operations, ensure safety compliance, and coordinate with project management.

Requirements:

  • 5+ years construction experience
  • Leadership and team management skills
  • Safety certification preferred
  • Municipal infrastructure experience an asset
Apply Now

Topman

Field Operations • Skilled Trade

Assist in field operations for municipal infrastructure projects. Support crew coordination and ensure project specifications are met.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years construction experience
  • Strong communication skills
  • Valid driver's license
  • Ability to work in team environment
Apply Now

Pipelayers

Skilled Trade • Infrastructure

Install and maintain water, sanitary, and storm pipeline systems. Work with modern equipment and follow municipal specifications.

Requirements:

  • Pipelaying experience preferred
  • Physical fitness required
  • Safety training an asset
  • Willingness to learn and advance
Apply Now

Equipment Operators

Heavy Equipment • Skilled Trade

Operate excavators, dozers, wheel loaders, and packers for municipal infrastructure projects. Multiple positions available.

Available Positions:

  • Mainline Excavator Operators
  • Side Hoe Excavator Operators
  • Dozer Operators
  • Wheel Loader Operators
  • Packer Operators
